# Discovery / Application Framework

**DECISION-MAKING REFERENCE ONLY. Does not render anything.**

How to structure the application flow and any qualification
conversations. Premium signal without interrogation.

---

## Rules

- Never start with a long questionnaire.
- Never ask for bank access in the application.
- Never request financial documents upfront.
- Never make the user feel interrogated.
- Apply in under 3 minutes total.

---

## Application steps

### Step 1 — Fit check

- Name
- Email
- Business name
- Monthly revenue range (chips, not free text):
  - Under $30K/mo (likely not a fit — surface free templates)
  - $30K–$75K/mo
  - $75K–$150K/mo
  - $150K–$250K/mo
  - $250K+/mo (route to a different intake)
- What are you trying to understand about your numbers? (short text)
- CTA: *Continue to Business Context*

### Step 2 — Business context

- Industry (chips: Agency, Clinic, Trades, Restaurant, Consultant, E-commerce, Local Service, Professional Firm, Other)
- Team size (chips: 1–3, 4–10, 11–20, 21–30, 30+)
- Current bookkeeping/accounting setup (QuickBooks + bookkeeper, QuickBooks alone, Xero, spreadsheets only, CPA only, none)
- Biggest financial frustration (short text)
- Currently using spreadsheets? (yes/no)
- CTA: *Continue to Fit Review*

### Step 3 — Readiness

- Comfortable with $1,500/month? (yes / maybe / no)
- Willing to review financials in a monthly one-hour call? (yes/no)
- What would make this valuable for you? (short text)
- CTA: *Submit Application*

### Confirmation

*Your application has been received. If the Monthly Finance Desk looks
like a fit, we'll follow up with next steps.*

No automatic "you're in" energy. The premium signal comes from
selectivity.

---

## Disqualification handling

Don't reject hard. Route soft.

- Under $30K/mo or "no" to $1,500 → *We don't think the Monthly Finance
  Desk is the right fit yet. Start with the free template library and
  apply again when the business has grown.*
- "No" to monthly review → *The Monthly Finance Desk depends on a
  recurring conversation. If that isn't a fit, the free templates may
  still help.*

---

## Discovery call (after qualification)

If the application reads green, the first call is a 30-minute fit
conversation, not a sales pitch. Agenda:

1. What is the business doing right now? (5 min)
2. What does the current finance setup look like? (10 min)
3. What decisions are coming up in the next 90 days? (10 min)
4. How the Monthly Finance Desk would handle those. (5 min)

End with: *We'll follow up by end of day with whether we think this
is a fit, and how onboarding would work.*
