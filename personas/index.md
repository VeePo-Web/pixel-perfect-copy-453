# Monthly Finance Desk — Persona Library

**DECISION-MAKING REFERENCE ONLY. Does not render anything.**

These docs are the agent's brief for the Monthly Finance Desk brand
(category: Financial Clarity-as-a-Service). Every copy, design, UX,
SEO, and conversion decision should be filtered through them.

The site sells financial control, interpretation, confidence, and
recurring rhythm — not spreadsheets, not AI, not dashboards.

## Read order

1. **`strategic-narrative.md`** — master conversion-architecture doc.
   Read this before any copy work. Contains the belief chain, hero
   emotional arc, hero variants A–D, word-by-word headline
   psychology, canonical sample briefing output, and the "must-not-say" list.
2. **`ideal-customer.md`** — Cashflow-Clarity Claire in depth. Internal
   monologue, hidden emotional state ladder, objection map, awareness stages.
3. **`brand-identity-architect.md`** — positioning, voice, mission/vision,
   phrases to use/avoid, and the explicit Goldman Sachs disclosure rule.
4. **`market-research.md`** — the "between three worlds" gap analysis.
5. Everything else (visual, motion, nav, footer, SEO, perf, engineering, responsive).

## Public-positioning rule (read before any copy work)

The phrase "Goldman Sachs" appears in internal positioning material
("mini Goldman Sachs-style finance desk"). It must never appear in
public-facing copy, meta tags, OG images, social posts, or ad copy.
Use **"institutional-style financial clarity"** or **"investment-bank-style
financial intelligence"** instead. See `brand-identity-architect.md`
for the full disclosure rule.
